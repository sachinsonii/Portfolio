About Me

Computer Science researcher specializing in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence with 3+ years of experience developing innovative solutions to complex computational problems.

I work at the intersection of theoretical computer science and practical software engineering, combining a strong foundation in algorithms with hands-on experience in AI/ML development. My expertise extends to building scalable AI-driven systems, optimizing machine learning models, and contributing to open-source projects that push the boundaries of innovation.

Beyond technical work, I actively engage with the developer community through conferences and collaborative research. I am passionate about open science, believing that shared knowledge accelerates progress and makes cutting-edge technology more accessible to everyone.

My Field

My research and professional work primarily focuses on the following areas:

  • Machine Learning & AI: Developing novel algorithms and frameworks for supervised and unsupervised learning tasks, with special emphasis on explainable AI systems.
  • Computer Vision: Researching efficient object detection and image segmentation techniques for resource-constrained environments.
  • Natural Language Processing: Building systems for text analysis, summarization, and language understanding with applications in information retrieval.

My approach combines rigorous theoretical analysis with practical implementation, ensuring that my research contributes both to the academic body of knowledge and to developing useful software solutions. I'm particularly interested in bridging the gap between theoretical advances in machine learning and their real-world applications.
